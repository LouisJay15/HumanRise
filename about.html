Our story

Recruitment with a human touch.

We started HumanRise on a simple belief: hiring is about people, not paperwork. Behind every CV is a person with ambitions, and behind every vacancy is a team trying to grow. Our job is to bring the two together — properly.

Today we place candidates across 18 sectors and support businesses with end-to-end HR, all grounded in POPIA-compliant, ethical practice.
